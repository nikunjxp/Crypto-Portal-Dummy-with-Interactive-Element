Crypto Queries Unleashed

What is cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is digital money that’s decentralized, meaning no one controls it. Think of it as cash for the internet!

How do I buy crypto?

You can buy crypto on exchanges like Coinbase or Binance. Just don’t forget your wallet!

Is crypto safe to invest in?

Like a rollercoaster, it’s thrilling but risky! Do your homework before jumping in.

What’s blockchain technology?

Blockchain is the tech behind crypto, a secure digital ledger that records transactions. It’s like a super secure diary!

Can I mine cryptocurrency at home?

Sure, if you have a powerful computer and a lot of electricity! But be prepared for some serious heat.
